How to Prevent Hair Shedding and Restore Your Crowning Glory

Hair shedding is something we all experience on a daily basis, whether we notice it or not. While it might be more apparent for those with longer hair, it’s normal to lose between 80 to 100 hairs in a single day. The average life cycle of hair begins with a growth phase called the anagen phase. This is followed by a transition phase (catagen) and then a rest phase known as the telogen phase.
